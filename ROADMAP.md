# NEO SF: Master Circuit - Development Roadmap

## ✅ Phase 1–3: The Foundation & Club Circuit (COMPLETED)
- [x] **Core Battle Engine**: Sync-Energy mana system, full turn structure (START → MAIN → BATTLE → END), hand/field management, and 3-prize win condition.
- [x] **Six Creature Types**: Pulse, Bloom, Tide, Alloy, Veil, and Current — each with unique keywords and a full weakness cycle (Pulse→Tide→Alloy→Veil→Current→Bloom→Pulse).
- [x] **50+ Card Library**: Multiple card sets (Metro Pulse, Neon Echo, Garden Shift, Bayline Current, Crown Circuit, Promo) with evolution chains, named attacks, and passive abilities (Swift, Guard, Regen, Boost, Armor, Evasive).
- [x] **AI Opponent**: Rule-based AI that evaluates playable cards by attack power and mana availability.
- [x] **Babylon.js 3D**: Interactive apartment hub and battle arena rendered with Babylon.js 6.
- [x] **Master Circuit Overworld**: Six explorable districts (Sunset Terminal, Bayline Wharf, Market Central, Neon Mission, Redwood Heights, Crown Hall) with time-of-day NPC scheduling.
- [x] **Visual Novel Engine**: Plugin-driven, renderer-agnostic VN engine with JSON scripts, conditional branching, choice trees, EventBus, and PluginManager architecture.
- [x] **The Medal System**: Grand Medals tied to district progression and Club Master dialogue loops.
- [x] **Tournament System**: Five tiers from Rookie to the endless Unlimited Crown Gauntlet, with bracket generation and prestige rewards.
- [x] **Economy & Boutique**: Currency-based Card Shop with booster packs (5-card pulls, weighted rarity), direct singles, and cosmetic purchases.
- [x] **60-Card Deck Builder**: Archetype filtering, name search, and live deck statistics.
- [x] **Social IDs & Profile**: Three-tab profile (Dossier / Inventory / Sync Analytics) with XP, level, win/loss record, faction reputation, and trainer relationships.
- [x] **System Infrastructure**: Full Save/Load via localStorage, AudioManager (BGM + SFX + TTS), and dev console diagnostics.

## 🏗️ Phase 4: "The Heart of the Circuit" (IN PROGRESS)
- [ ] **Advanced AI Personalities**: Unique "Sync Behaviors" for each Club Master — Valerious' calculated patience vs. Vex's high-pressure aggression — driven by per-trainer deck archetypes.
- [ ] **Deck Builder Upgrade**: Drag-and-drop card reordering and filtered "Archetype Evolution" views showing full evolution chains at a glance.
- [ ] **Cinematic Audio Polish**: Full BGM Manager implementation with district-specific atmospheric tracks and smooth crossfade transitions.
- [ ] **Quest Log & Medal Case**: A dedicated UI panel tracking seasonal events, Grand Medal progress, and trainer relationship milestones.
- [ ] **NPC Depth Pass**: Expand NPC dialogue pools beyond starter beats; add rivalry escalation arcs for Kaizen and other key trainers.

## ⚡ Phase 5: "Sync Awakening" & Post-Game (FUTURE)
- [ ] **Sync-Pet Care System**: A Tamagotchi-style loop launched from the Apartment Hub. Feed, groom, and bond with your primary partner to unlock 'Sync-Boost' battle multipliers and hidden evolution paths.
- [ ] **Archetype Evolution**: Morph cards into higher-sync variants based on XP and Bond level — the full 'Awakening' system powering end-game progression.
- [ ] **Prismatic Foil Crafting**: Personalized card styling with holographic field effects and animated card borders.
- [ ] **Expanded Card Sets**: New sets introducing remaining type synergies, cross-type evolution branches, and Promo-exclusive legendaries.
- [ ] **Steam Deck & Controller Optimization**: Full gamepad binding for high-velocity circuit navigation and battle input.
- [ ] **P2P Local Terminal Duel**: Direct maglev-link duels between two players over a local network connection.
