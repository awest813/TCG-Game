# NEO SF: Champion Circuit - Development Roadmap

## ✅ Phase 1-3: The Foundation & District Climb (COMPLETED)
- [x] **Core Battle Engine**: Sync Rate-based mana system, turn-based logic, and hand/field management.
- [x] **District Navigation**: Established the "Narrative Navigation" overworld with 10+ authored sub-scenes.
- [x] **Visual Overhaul**: Implementation of Holo-Projectors, Scanlines, and Glassmorphic HUDs.
- [x] **Social & Retail**: Integrated NPC dialogue loops (Maya, Rin, Vex) and District Boutiques (Bloom, Arcade).
- [x] **System Infrastructure**: Full Save/Load, Options Menu, and Keyword Tooltip systems.

---

## 🏗️ Phase 4: Expansion & "The Deep Circuit" (IN PROGRESS)
- [ ] **Advanced AI Personalities**: Unique "Duel Behaviors" for each major NPC boss (e.g., Zeno's patience vs. Rin's speed).
- [ ] **Deck Builder Upgrade**: Drag-and-drop support and filtered "Archetype Views."
- [ ] **Expanded Card Pool**: Launch the "Alloy Core" and "Crown Relic" card sets.
- [ ] **Quest Log UI**: A dedicated tracker for ongoing district events and character social links.

---

## ⚡ Phase 5: "Launch Window" & Post-Campaign (FUTURE)
- [ ] **The Gauntlet (Post-Game)**: Infinite survival mode at the Civic Crown Arena.
- [ ] **Card Foil Crafting**: Burn common cards to generate "Rare Prismatic" field variants.
- [ ] **Controller/Steam Deck Support**: Optimized input handling for high-speed navigation.
- [ ] **Arena Local Multi-player**: LAN-based duel support for local circuit play.
