# NEO SF: Master Circuit - Development Roadmap

## ✅ Phase 1-3: The Foundation & Club Circuit (COMPLETED)
- [x] **Core Battle Engine**: Sync-Energy mana system, turn-based logic, and hand/field management.
- [x] **Master Circuit Overworld**: Narrative Navigation with "Postcard Polish" (Ken Burns & Particle effects).
- [x] **The Medal System**: Integrated 8 Grand Medals and corresponding Club Master dialogue loops.
- [x] **Tournament Gauntlet**: Tiered entry-fee tournaments and the Endless "Unlimited Crown" gauntlet.
- [x] **Economy & Social**: High-end Boutique shopping, Single Card acquisition, and XP-based Profile IDs.
- [x] **System Infrastructure**: Full Save/Load and "Retro RPG" options profiling.

---

## 🏗️ Phase 4: "The Heart of the Circuit" (IN PROGRESS)
- [ ] **Advanced AI Personalities**: Unique "Sync Behaviors" for each Club Master (Valerious' patience vs. Vex's aggression).
- [ ] **Deck Builder Upgrade**: Drag-and-drop support and filtered "Archetype Evolution" views.
- [ ] **Cinematic Audio Polish**: Full implementation of the BGM Manager with district-specific atmospheric tracks.
- [ ] **Quest Log & Medal Case**: A dedicated UI tracker for seasonal events and Grand Medal progression.

---

## ⚡ Phase 5: "Sync Awakening" & Post-Game (FUTURE)
- [ ] **Sync-Pet Care System**: A Tamagotchi-style gameplay loop launched from the Sync-Den. Feed, groom, and bond with your primary partner to unlock 'Sync-Boost' battle multipliers and hidden evolution paths.
- [ ] **Archetype Evolution**: Morph cards into higher-sync variants based on XP and Bond level (The 'Awakening' system).
- [ ] **Prismatic Foil Crafting**: Personalized card styling and holographic field effects.
- [ ] **Steam Deck & Controller Optimization**: Full gamepad binding for high-velocity circuit navigation.
- [ ] **P2P Local Terminal Duel**: Direct maglev-link duels between two players over local network.
